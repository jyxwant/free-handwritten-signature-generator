Frequently Asked Questions

How can I use the PNG signature in Word or Google Docs?

Once you download the PNG file, simply open your document, click "Insert" > "Image", and select your signature file. Because it has a transparent background, it will look like you signed directly on the paper. You can resize it to fit the signature line perfectly.

Is this legally binding?

In many jurisdictions, a handwritten electronic signature (e-signature) on a document carries the same weight as a wet ink signature. However, for highly sensitive legal documents requiring audit trails and certificates (digital signatures), you might need specialized software. For everyday contracts, invoices, and letters, this tool is perfect.

Why is it better than scanning a paper signature?

Scanning a paper signature often results in a gray, shadowy background that looks unprofessional on digital documents. Our online signature creator produces a crisp, clean digital ink line with a perfectly transparent background every time.
