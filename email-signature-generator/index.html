  3. 3

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a handwritten signature to Gmail?

First, create your handwritten signature using our free signature maker. Download the transparent PNG, then upload it here. Your signature will appear in the preview and will be included when you copy for Gmail.

Why isn't my signature image showing in Gmail?

Gmail requires signature images to be hosted online. For the image to display, you'll need to either: (1) use an image hosting service and paste the URL, or (2) insert the image directly in Gmail's signature editor after pasting the text.

Can I use this signature in Outlook?

Yes! Click "Copy for Gmail" (it works for all email clients), then paste into Outlook's signature settings. In Outlook, go to File > Options > Mail > Signatures.

Is this really free with no hidden costs?

Absolutely! This tool is 100% free. No sign-up, no email required, no watermarks, no limits. We believe professional email signatures should be accessible to everyone.
