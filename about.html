About Signature Sketch

Our Story

Signature Sketch was created by an independent developer with a simple goal: to provide a free, easy-to-use, and privacy-focused tool for creating digital handwritten signatures.

I noticed that many people struggle with adding their signature to digital documents. Existing solutions were either too complicated, required expensive subscriptions, or raised privacy concerns by uploading sensitive signature data to external servers.

Why I Built This

I believe that something as simple as creating a digital signature shouldn't require:

  • Creating an account or signing up
  • Paying monthly subscription fees
  • Uploading your personal signature to unknown servers
  • Installing software or browser extensions

That's why Signature Sketch is completely free, runs 100% in your browser, and never uploads any data.

Our Commitment
