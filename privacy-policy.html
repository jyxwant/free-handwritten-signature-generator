Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 31, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Signature Sketch ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our free online signature generator tool at signaturesketch.tech.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Our signature tool operates entirely within your web browser. Specifically:

  • Your signature data is never uploaded to our servers
  • No personal information such as name, email, or phone number is collected
  • No account registration is required to use our service
  • No cookies are used for tracking purposes

3. How Our Tool Works

Signature Sketch is a client-side application. This means:

  • All signature drawing and processing happens locally in your browser
  • When you download your signature, the file is generated directly on your device
  • Once you close or refresh the page, your signature data is permanently deleted from browser memory
  • We have no ability to access, view, or recover any signatures you create

4. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.
  • Vercel Analytics: For anonymous website usage statistics (page views, not personal data).

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data or signatures, there is no risk of your signature data being compromised on our servers. Your signatures exist only in your browser's memory during your session.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. Since we do not collect personal information from any users, this applies universally.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
